Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our services and process

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity, but most Excel automation projects take 2-4 weeks, while custom software development typically requires 6-12 weeks.

Do you provide training and support?

Yes, all our projects include comprehensive documentation and training sessions. We also offer ongoing support packages to ensure your team can effectively use the solutions we build.

What data sources can you work with?

We can integrate with virtually any data source including Excel files, CSV, SQL databases, APIs, cloud services (Google Sheets, Airtable), and business applications like Shopify, QuickBooks, and Salesforce.

Is my data secure with you?

Absolutely. We follow strict data security protocols, use encrypted connections, and can sign NDAs. Your data remains confidential and is never shared with third parties.
